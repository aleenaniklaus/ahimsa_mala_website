NOAA has our hearts

Ahimsa Mala's designer, Aleena has a long time relationship for The National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration, aka NOAA. Volenteering to help overlook the visitor center and count whale sightings of the shores of the Big Island of Hawaii planted a seed when she was a child. Now, with every purchase on Ahimsamala.org, $1 goes to NOAA. We hope that a larger margin will go to NOAA in the future as the buisness continues to grow. If you would like more donated to NOAA, please specify in the comments section. Mahalo for making an impact on our sacred lands and waters.
